The Hidden Magical Space Nebula Club

The Club

The Hidden Magical Space Nebula Club is our local free group where young people can learn how to Photograph great spce nebulas and other Astonomical Objects seeen from our planet and more.

The Nebula Club is led by volunteers and it depends on the community to run because it is absolutely free.

To The Parents

All Parents must attend their first child's first class to sign the parental permission. Parents of under 12 must stay on the premises at all times.

Please be advised that places will be limited to ticket holders only: One Ticket Per Child.
